What is AC Service & Repair?

Professional air conditioner service covering gas refilling, cooling issue diagnosis, filter and coil cleaning, drainage clearing, compressor checks, and installation or uninstallation for split, window, and cassette AC units.

Who needs this service?

Homeowners whose AC isn't cooling properly, is leaking water, making unusual noise, or is due for its seasonal service before summer; also businesses maintaining office and commercial AC units.

Benefits of Professional AC Service & Repair

  • Lower electricity bills — a poorly maintained AC can use significantly more power to cool the same room
  • Better air quality — clean filters and coils reduce dust and odour circulating in the room
  • Fewer breakdowns — routine servicing catches small issues (low gas, worn parts) before they become expensive repairs
  • Longer compressor life — regular cleaning reduces strain on the unit

Common Problems We Fix

  • AC running but not cooling (usually low refrigerant gas or a dirty condenser coil)
  • Water leaking from the indoor unit (blocked drain pipe)
  • Unusual rattling, buzzing, or grinding noise
  • AC switching off on its own (often a sensor or gas pressure issue)
  • Bad smell when the AC starts (mould or bacteria buildup in the coil/filter)

Best time to book

Ideally get a general service done just before the summer season (March–April) and once more before winter storage. If your AC is not cooling right now, that is a same-visit repair, not something to wait on.
