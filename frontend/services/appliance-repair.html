What is Appliance Repair?

Repair and servicing for major home appliances — washing machines, refrigerators, microwaves, water purifiers, chimneys, and geysers — including diagnosis, part replacement, and routine maintenance.

Who needs this service?

Anyone whose appliance has stopped working, is making noise, leaking, or underperforming (e.g. a fridge not cooling, a washing machine not spinning, a geyser not heating).

Benefits of Professional Appliance Repair

  • Often far cheaper than replacing the appliance outright
  • Restores full energy efficiency (a struggling compressor or motor uses more power)
  • Extends the working life of the appliance
  • Genuine diagnosis before any part is replaced, so you are not paying for guesswork

Common Problems We Fix

  • Washing machine not spinning, draining, or leaking water
  • Refrigerator not cooling or making unusual noise
  • Geyser not heating water or tripping the MCB
  • Water purifier with low flow or a persistent alert light
  • Chimney not pulling smoke/odour effectively

Best time to book

As soon as you notice the issue — most appliance faults do not fix themselves and can sometimes damage other parts of the unit if left running incorrectly.
