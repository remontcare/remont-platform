What is Interior Design?

End-to-end interior design and execution — modular kitchens, wardrobes, false ceilings, wall finishes, lighting design, and full home or office interior fit-outs, from concept to handover.

Who needs this service?

Homeowners moving into a new flat, planning a kitchen or wardrobe upgrade, or businesses fitting out a new office space.

Benefits of Professional Interior Design

  • A single point of contact for design, material selection, and execution instead of coordinating multiple contractors
  • Better use of available space with custom modular solutions
  • Design guidance from someone who also has to build and deliver it, reducing gaps between plan and execution
  • Clear, itemised costing before work begins

Common Problems We Fix

  • Kitchen layout that wastes storage space
  • Outdated or damaged wardrobes and cabinetry
  • Ceiling that needs false ceiling work for lighting or aesthetics
  • A new flat that needs full interior execution before move-in
  • Office space that needs a professional fit-out

Best time to book

Ideally planned 4–8 weeks before you need to move in or open the space, since design, material sourcing, and execution take time. For a single unit like a wardrobe, timelines are much shorter.
